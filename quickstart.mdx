## Overview

Jay Hanson streamlines your business expansion into Eastern Europe, Central Asia, and the Caucasus. As part of Schneider Group, you gain access to experts in legal, tax, accounting, HR, and ERP solutions across 18 offices in 15 countries. Start by scheduling a consultation to assess your needs and kick off onboarding.

<Callout kind="info">
Before beginning, ensure you have:
- Your company's expansion goals defined (e.g., new subsidiary in Poland or Kazakhstan)
- Key contacts for decision-making
- Basic financial projections for the target market
</Callout>

## Assess Expansion Requirements

During the call, discuss your specific needs. Jay Hanson assesses risks, costs, and timelines for markets like Ukraine, Georgia, or Uzbekistan.

| Requirement | Description | Example Documents |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Market Analysis | Target country evaluation | Business plan PDF |
| Legal Structure | Branch vs. subsidiary | Incorporation docs |
| Tax & Compliance | Local regulations | Financial statements |
| HR Setup | Employee contracts | Org chart |

Prepare `{financial_projections}` and `{market_research}` files for faster assessment.
